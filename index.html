Object Detection With Few-Shot Learning (Final Year BTP)

In Few-Shot Learning ,a classifier must generalize to new classes which is not observed in the training set, given just a few samples of each new class, we use prototype networks. We measure the distance between prototype representations of each class. Prototypical networks develop an under- standing of the metric space . They provide outstanding results and represent a simpler inductive bias than contemporary few-shot learning methods.
